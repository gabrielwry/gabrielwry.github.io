Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-06-02

Weaver ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS app ("Weaver" or the "App").

By using Weaver, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

We may collect personally identifiable information, such as:

  • Name or username
  • Email address (for account creation or recovery)
  • Profile picture (optional)

b. Usage Data

We automatically collect information about how the app is accessed and used, such as:

  • Device type and OS version
  • IP address
  • App usage patterns and interactions

c. Content Data

We collect and store content you create or contribute to within the app (e.g., stories, comments, collaboration sessions).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Provide and maintain the app
  • Enable collaborative storytelling features
  • Improve the app’s functionality and user experience
  • Send support and service-related communications
  • Detect and prevent abuse or violations of terms

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your data. We may share information with:

  • Service providers (e.g., analytics, cloud storage)
  • Law enforcement if required by law or to protect our rights
  • Other users, if you choose to publicly share content

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (e.g., Firebase, Google Analytics for Firebase, Apple StoreKit) that collect, monitor, and analyze usage to help us improve our services. These third parties have their own privacy policies.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the app and comply with legal obligations. You may delete your account or request data removal by contacting us.

6. Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

Weaver is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data. Contact us at [me@gabrielwry.com] to exercise your rights.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app and/or on our website.
